What are GLP-1 treatments?

GLP-1 receptor agonists mimic the GLP-1 hormone, which regulates blood sugar and appetite. Initially developed for type 2 diabetes, they also promote significant weight loss by:

Enhancing Satiety

Helping you feel full longer, which can lead to reduced calorie intake.

Improving Blood Sugar Control

Assisting in maintaining healthy blood glucose levels.

Supporting Cardiovascular Health

Offering potential heart health benefits.

Our GLP-1 Treatment Options

We provide two primary GLP-1 treatment options, each designed to cater to different needs:

Semaglutide

Administration

A once-weekly subcutaneous injection.

Efficacy

Clinical studies have shown that Semaglutide can lead to substantial weight loss, with patients experiencing an average of 15% weight reduction over 68 weeks.

Additional Benefits

Beyond weight loss, Semaglutide may improve glycemic control and offer cardiovascular protection.

Tirzepatide

Administration

A once-weekly subcutaneous injection.

Efficacy

Research indicates that Tirzepatide may result in even greater weight loss, with some studies reporting up to a 22.4% reduction in body weight over a year.

Additional Benefits

Tirzepatide not only aids in weight management but also enhances blood sugar regulation and supports heart health.
